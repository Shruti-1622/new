04 / FAQ

Got Questions?

What is HackHub?
HackHub is a platform where you can discover hackathons, find teammates, register for events, and explore winning projects—all in one place.
Can I participate without a team?
Yes. You can register as an individual and use HackHub to find teammates before the event begins.
Who can use HackHub?
Students, developers, designers, entrepreneurs, and anyone interested in hackathons can use HackHub.
Why use HackHub instead of searching hackathons manually?
HackHub brings hackathons, registrations, team discovery, and winner showcases together in one place, saving you hours of searching.
